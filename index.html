Body Building

Overview

Exercises are a great way to maintain a healthy lifestyle. There are several websites and videos available online on this subject.

Different exercises and workout plans for different areas of the body, which can be incorporated in your daily routine.

However,bear in mind that, there are no exercises that target specific areas of the body .

5 Common Exercises To Tone Your Muscles

In no particular order, below is a list of 5 common exercises out there:

  • Lunges
  • Push-ups
  • Squats
  • Burpees
  • Planks

Focus Areas

Top 5 areas which require frequent exercises include the following:
  1. Upper Body
  2. Lower Body
  3. Core
  4. Flexibility
